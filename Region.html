Demacia


Demacia, the radiant kingdom of honor and order—a land where justice shines bright, and the ideals of chivalry and nobility are held aloft as guiding stars. Yet, beneath its gleaming surface, cracks in its perfection hint at deeper struggles.


Noxus


Noxus, a land where ambition reigns supreme and strength defines destiny. Unlike other kingdoms built on traditions or lofty ideals, Noxus thrives on power, adaptability, and the relentless pursuit of greatness. To outsiders, it may appear ruthless or barbaric, but to its people, Noxus is a beacon of opportunity and unity—a place where anyone can rise, should they possess the will.


Piltover


Piltover, the shining City of Progress, a place where innovation and ambition converge to create wonders that defy imagination. It is a city of gleaming towers and bustling workshops, driven by the boundless energy of invention. Piltover is a hub of trade, technology, and culture, earning its title as the beating heart of progress in Runeterra.


Shurima


Shurima, a land of golden sands and ancient glory, where history is written in shifting dunes and crumbling ruins. Once the seat of a mighty empire that spanned continents, Shurima now stands as a land of fractured legacies, with whispers of its past echoing through the desert winds. Let me take you to the heart of Shurima, where power, ambition, and destiny intertwine.
